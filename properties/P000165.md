A space $X$ is strongly zero-dimensional if for any closed disjoint $E, F\subseteq X$ there exists clopen set $V$ such that $E\subseteq V, F\subseteq V^c$.

See section 6.2 of {{doi:10.1007/978-3-030-22232-1}}.
