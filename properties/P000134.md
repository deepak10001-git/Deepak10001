An ordered space $(X, <)$ with at least two points such that
1. If $A\subset X$ is bounded above then it has a least upper bound (supremum).
2. If $x<y$ then there is $z$ such that $x<z<y$.

Defined on page 153 of {{mr:3728284}}.
