Suppose not. Then suppose $F$ is an open set with $|F|>1$ minimal. 
Choose $x,y\in F$. There must be some open $U$ containing exactly one of $x,y$. 
Then $U\cap F$ is open and $|U\cap F|<|F|$, contradiction.
