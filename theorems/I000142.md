Given any cozero $U \subset X$, $X \setminus \overline U$ contains a maximal pairwise-disjoint collection of cozero sets $\mathcal{V}$. $\mathcal{V}$ is countable by CCC, so $\cup \mathcal{V}$ is a cozero complement for $U$.
