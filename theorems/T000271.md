Let $\{U_n\}$ be a countable basis for $X$, and $S\subseteq X$.  For each $n \in \omega$ for which $S\cap U_n\neq \emptyset$, choose $x_n \in S\cap U_n$. Then $\{x_n\}$ is a countable dense subset of $S$.
