Consider its Kolmogorov quotient $\mathrm{KQ}(X)$, it is {P3}, thus {P100}. Now it follows by {P208} that every subspace is closed, which implies {P52}.

Therefore, $\mathrm{KQ}(X)$ is {P52}, and we obtain that $X$ is {P185}.
