      name: Answer to "Is there a first countable, $T_1$, weakly Lindelof, sequentially compact space which is not also compact?"

$\{(\alpha+1)\setminus F:F\in[\alpha]^{<\aleph_0}\}$ is a countable base at
each $\alpha<\omega_1$.
