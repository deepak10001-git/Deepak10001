The diagonal $\Delta=\{(x,x):x\in\mathbb Q^*\}\subseteq X$ is homeomorphic to $\mathbb Q^*$ ({S29}), which is {P16}.  But it is not closed in $X=\mathbb Q^*\times\mathbb Q^*$ since $\mathbb Q^*$ is not {P3}.  This shows that $X$ is not {P100}.

See {{mathse:632320}}.
