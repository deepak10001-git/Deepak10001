Suppose that $x,y \in X$ are distinct.

* If $x \neq p$, then $\{ x \}$ is an open neighborhood of $x$ not containing $y$.
* If $x = p$, then $X \setminus \{ y \}$ is an open neighborhood of $x$ not containing $y$.
