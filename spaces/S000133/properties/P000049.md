Let $x_n$ be a sequence of points converging to $p$ such that $x_n\neq p$. Then $\{x_n:n<\omega\}$ is open, but $\overline{\{x_n:n<\omega\}}=\{p\}\cup\{x_n:n<\omega\}$ is not open, since every neighborhood of $p$ is uncountable.
