$(X, \tau^{*})$ is an expansion of $(X,\tau)$ since every open set in $\tau$ contains a $\tau^{*}$ neighborhood of each of its points. Thus $(X, \tau^{*})$ is $T_{2 \frac{1}{2}}$.
