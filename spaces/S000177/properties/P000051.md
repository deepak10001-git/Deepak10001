Every point $a_{\alpha\beta}$ and $b_{\alpha\beta}$ is isolated in $X$.  And any set not containing these points is a subset of $\{a,b\}\cup\{c_\gamma:\gamma<\omega_1\}$, which is a discrete subspace of $X$.
